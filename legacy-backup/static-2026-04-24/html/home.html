Your Luggage, Our Responsibility.

Book licensed Koolie at Indian railway stations — affordable, verified and trackable.

eKoolie connects travellers to licensed coolies (Koolie) so you can move through stations without the burden of heavy luggage. Quick booking, transparent fares, and verified Koolie for peace of mind.

Book a Koolie

How it works

Simple three-step booking to get a Koolie at your platform.

Search Station

Type your arrival station or select from recent stations to find available Koolie nearby.

Select Verified Koolie

Choose from licensed Koolie with ratings, ID verification and fare per bag — or let eKoolie auto-assign one.

Relax — Koolie Arrives

Track your Koolie in-app, confirm arrival, pay securely or cash on service, and leave a rating afterward.
